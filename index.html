Features

Real-time structuring

The AI feature in the background will automatically recognize the content (title, body, image, video, skatch) and it will structure the note into a usable and printable ready paper.

Simultaneous writing and voice transcription

One can either work with the AI powered voice transcription, type down the notes manually, or it can run the AI powered voice assistant while manually typing.

Group collaboration

A group of students or coworkers can collaborate on the same document simultaneously.

Scoring

Onofo will have a scoring feature that will measure the contribution in % when multiple people work on the same note and provide a rank to keep collaborators engaged and challenged.

Time Assistant

Onofo will have an integrated AI powered Time Assistant, that upon completion of the note will provide insight about how much time one will need to invest to review/study the notes.

Cross Platform

Onofo it’s a cross platform, which means you can either use it within a browser or mobile and tablet (iOS and Android).
